SHEILA GAYNOR

I am a Senior Manager of Statistical Genetics in the Regeneron Genetics Center. I develop statistical genetic and machine learning methods and perform large-scale analyses to drive genetic discovery.

I previously was a postdoctoral fellow in statistical genetics with Xihong Lin and completed my PhD, "Statistical Methods for Integratively Characterizing Genetic and Genomic Data," under the advisement of John Quackenbush and Xihong Lin in the Biostatistics department at the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health.
