SHEILA GAYNOR

I am a Senior Manager of Statistical Genetics at the Regeneron Genetics Center, where I develop statistical genetics and machine learning methods and lead large-scale analyses to advance genetic discovery.

I previously completed a postdoctoral fellowship in statistical genetics with Xihong Lin. I completed my PhD in Biostatistics from the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health, where my dissertation, Statistical Methods for Integratively Characterizing Genetic and Genomic Data, was jointly advised by John Quackenbush and Xihong Lin.
